  Hi, really nice to meet you and welcome to my website. 🐣

  I am a 1st year PhD candidate at the National University of Singapore, Department of Psychology. Prior to that, in 2021, I graduated from the University of Bristol with an upper-second-class honors degree in psychology. A year later, I received a master's degree in behavioral and data science from the University of Warwick with distinction.

  My past research generally enquired the potential effects that some endogenous factors such as culture and self-perception may have on human cognitive ability. More broadly, I am also keen on applying computational approaches to understand complex cognitive and societal phenomena in both human and non-human species. I am currently supervised by Dr Elias Garcia-Pelegrin, and we share a common interest in the study of evolutionary anthropology.
